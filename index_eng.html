Welcome to the "UNIREM" website

The UNIREM firm makes for food production packing sponges and knives of different types: gear, zigzag, cutting, knives like flowpack of any shapes and sizes. In the manufacture of knives high-quality special steels are used. HRC 56 ... 62. Prices are reasonable. We guarantee the quality of knives.

We also make confectionery knives: SPS, HBTS, Fudji, Chempak, Sig, Siat, Rovema, Acma, Hassia, Richarelli, Sakmi, Sapal, Mashek (BH-05, BH-07), Nagema (EU-7,EU-8, EF-4, ED-10), Tehama, Mecpack, Imanpack, Carerra.
