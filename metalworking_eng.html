Metalworking

Grinding works

Grinding - the process of cutting metals using an abrasive tool, whose cutting element is grain. Grains have high hardness, heat resistance and sharp edges joined by special binders into grinding wheels, segments, heads, bars and skins; apply grains in the form of pastes and powders. Our specialists in metal processing, quality will perform grinding work of various species on modern equipment.

We perform:
  • -flat grinding;
  • -flat-carousel grinding;
  • -round-outer grinding;
  • -round-internal grinding.

Milling works

Milling operation in the production process of parts is very important. Thanks to this operation, it is possible to make exact dimensions of some elements of the part than when casting. The main tool for this operation is a milling cutter, rigidly mounted on the shaft of the milling machine. Due to the high speed of the cutter and representation of the workpiece at the desired angle, direction and speed, metal from the workpiece it is removed quite easily, and most importantly with fairly high accuracy.

The main types of milling operations:
  • -face milling (milling of large surfaces);
  • -end milling (grooves, hooks, grooves, pockets, wells).

Grinding works

Tool support in working condition is provided by timely and high-quality sharpening - grinding the working surfaces of the tool.

The main purpose of the tool sharpening process is as follows:
  • - ensuring optimal geometric parameters of the cutting part of the tool, contributing to increase its stability, as well as accuracy and processing performance;
  • - providing specified roughness limits of sharpened tool surfaces;
  • - preservation and restoration of cutting properties inherent in the tool, with minimally permissible changes in its surface layers;
  • - ensuring the economic exploitation of the instrument.

Gear cutting works

Gear cutting is a type of machining (machining). The company will fulfill any types of gear cutting works: cutting gears, cutting bevel gears, worm gears incl. globoid gearing, spline joints, chevron gearing other. Our organization has a powerful enough tooling area to perform gear cutting work according to customer drawings. For gear cutting operations, our workshop uses modular cutters and chisels. Depending on the gearing method, modular disk or finger milling cutters and gear cutting heads are used. for copying, gear cutting combs, worm cutters, cutters, gear cutting tools and cutter heads for run-in method. The disk mill is shaped and has a backed tooth, whose profile in the radial plane corresponds to the profile of the cavity of the cut gear.

Turning works

Turning is the machining of external and internal surfaces. rotation, including cylindrical and conical, cutting, cutting, chamfering, fillet processing, grooving, cutting internal and external threads on lathes.

Types of turning:
  • - turning - external surface treatment;
  • - boring - processing of internal surfaces;
  • - trimming - machining flat end surfaces;

Rubberizing shafts

"Unirem" offers professional rubberizing of shafts with high-quality raw materials. On any production (and in almost any field) there are units whose proper operation impossible without rubberized metal parts. It can be shafts, rollers, drums and much more. The main purpose of rubberizing is to protect a metal surface from destructive actions. the environment. It is a question of both chemical mixtures and simple mechanical friction. Elastic rubber resistant to mechanical deformation. In addition, the rubber coating reduces vibration, allowing reduce noise by almost a third. All together, it says one thing: rubberizing elements extends the life of the entire structure and significantly increases the efficiency of its work.



Our company rubberizes metal using several technologies:
  • - cold vulcanization
  • - hot vulcanization;
  • - bolted plates.

The team "Unirem" does not stand still. We are constantly exploring new technical developments and we ourselves create original mixtures of high-strength rubber on based on imported rubber of excellent quality. In addition, for higher adhesive indicators, our experts use high-quality foreign glue mixtures.
